Background

Pharmacy apps offer convenience by managing medication, comparing medicines, and browsing products. They enhance customer loyalty for pharmacies and are likely to become widely used in Lebanon due to the demand for healthcare-related apps.

Motivation

To address Lebanon's need for a dependable, user-friendly platform providing accurate information and efficient management of medicines. It aims to empower individuals with a trustworthy healthcare source

Posed Problem

The crisis in Lebanon has created healthcare challenges, including medication shortages and high prices. .
